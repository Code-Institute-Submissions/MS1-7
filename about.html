Behind AMH Beauty

AMHbeauty is owned by Ashleigh. Ashleigh is fully qualified, fully insured and has all the necessary regulation in place to practice. With Ashleigh, you are guaranteed to leave feeling beautiful.

AMHbeauty opened beginning of 2020 and has since been helping clients feel and look their best. Offering treatments from eyelash extensions, HD brows and brow lamination all the way to facial threading, mesotherapy and the no needle lip filler using the Hyaluron Pen.

Based in Dunholm, Lincolnshire.
